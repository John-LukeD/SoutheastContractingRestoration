At Southeast Contracting & Restoration, we take pride in delivering high-quality renovations and construction projects that enhance the beauty, functionality, and value of every home we work on. From full-scale remodels to detailed finishing touches, our skilled team is dedicated to bringing our clients' visions to life. Below, you'll find a showcase of some of our past projects, highlighting our expertise in drywall installation, flooring, painting, trim work, electrical upgrades, and complete home transformations. Whether it's modernizing interiors, expanding living spaces, or restoring older homes, we approach every project with precision, professionalism, and attention to detail. Take a look at our work and see how we turn renovation dreams into reality!

In this renovation, we transformed the space by removing the outdated tiled drop ceiling and replacing it with new drywall, extending the ceiling height from 8 feet to 10 feet for a more open and modern feel. The original wood paneling on the walls was removed and replaced with fresh drywall, creating a smooth and updated interior. On the right side of the room, we removed an old chimney and fireplace, making way for the addition of a second bathroom, significantly increasing the functionality of the home. The renovation also included all-new flooring, updated electrical wiring with new fixtures and outlets, and the installation of new trim, baseboards, and doorways. To enhance natural light and efficiency, brand-new windows were installed, along with a new door and door trim. The space was completed with a fresh coat of paint, giving it a bright and welcoming finish. This renovation was part of a full home transformation, improving both the aesthetics and functionality of the property.

One of our recent renovation projects involved a complete interior transformation, focusing on drywall installation, finishing, painting, flooring, and trim work. The process began with the installation of new drywall, followed by taping, mudding, and sanding the joints to create a seamless surface. Once the walls were prepped, they were primed and painted, instantly revitalizing the space with a fresh, modern look. In addition to the walls, we installed new flooring, enhancing both the durability and aesthetic appeal of the interior. To complete the renovation, new baseboards and trim were carefully fitted, adding a polished and refined finish to the space. The result is a beautifully updated room that feels brand new, demonstrating our commitment to quality craftsmanship and attention to detail in every project we take on.
