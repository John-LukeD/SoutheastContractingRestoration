Meet the Owner

Scott Austin is the owner of Southeast Contracting & Restoration, bringing years of expertise in both construction and finance. As a licensed general contractor, he has successfully led numerous renovation and building projects, ensuring quality craftsmanship in every job. In addition to his work in construction, Scott is also a licensed CPA at Bank of America, demonstrating his strong financial and business acumen. Beyond his professional life, he is a dedicated husband and father of three sons, balancing his passion for building homes with his commitment to family.
